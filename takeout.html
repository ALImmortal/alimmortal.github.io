Decide My Takeout - Asian Edition

Completed: Feb 23, 2022

Objective: Help users explore different kids of Asian cuisines by deciding a takeout for him/her to try. It will specifically ask for a cuisine he/she wants to choose and will randomly select a dish from a list of dishes I had created. Once the program provides a dish, it will also recommend an appetizer/dessert/drink to make their experience better!
