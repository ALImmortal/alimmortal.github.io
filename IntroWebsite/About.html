How did you get into Computer Science?

It all started... with Scratch

I first approached this in my freshman year of high school, now I didn't know any coding but the real fun didn't start until my junior year, where I was was enrolled to a Java course.

Coding became so fun for me that I would try to get my hands on any coding practice as much as I could while at school (including taking some of my teacher's worksheets for Advanced Placement Java)
